Pav Bhaji Recipe

Description

HEAVY BUTTER ALERT!

This dish is red and yellow in colour.Lots of vegetables.Looks saucy.Mumbai cuisine.But I like Vada Pav.

Ingredients

  • Pav
  • Tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Various Spices
  • Water

Steps

  1. Start by making the bhaji by adding vegetables,spices,water and salt.
  2. Let it boil.
  3. Add butter.
  4. On another pan make butter pav by adding butter and heating pav.
