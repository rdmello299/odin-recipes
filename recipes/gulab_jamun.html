Pav Bhaji Recipe

Description

HEAVY SUGAR ALERT!

This dish is brown balls in a pale,sugary liquid.Fried Balls.Popular sweet dish.Bengali sweet.But I like Rasgulla.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Elaichi
  • Cashews (optional)
  • Pista
  • Water

Steps

  1. Start by making the sweet liquid by adding sugar,water and elaichi.
  2. Let it boil.
  3. Let it cool down.
  4. In another vessel knead dough with flour,shape into balls and fry them.
  5. Immerse fried balls in sugar liquid.
